How it Works?

How is SarvM Making a Difference ?

All Stakeholders in Food Chain - Producers, Distributors, Retailers, Logistics Partners - are enabled to be self-reliant in managing their Sell, Purchase, Inventory, Credit and Customers

Pre-Order facilities and Insights on Customers will help Retailers, Distributors, Producers, Farmers to optimise inventory which in turn will lead to reduction of food wastage

Sellers avail Digitalised features like Management of - Credit, Inventory, Order, etc with ease and no extra cost. Buyers have the convenience of exploring wider choice, customisation of order & delivery.

Buyers at all levels are not constrained by number of Sellers and vice-versa. Buyers & Sellers get a wider choice set to Buy & Sell the Best Quality product at Best price.

Empowering Local Vendors by Digitalising the eco-system of Hyper-Local Commerce. End Consumers can enjoy the same neighbourhood shopping and take pride in helping Local Vendors grow.

Coming Soon

Delivery App

Food will be delivered from Seller to Buyer at all stages. It can be third party Delivery partners, or Delivery persons Trusted by the Seller / Buyer.

Distributor App

Wholesalers and Distributors will be empowered to buy from Producers and Sell to the Retailers. The Distributor App will be feature rich to help them become self-reliant.

Producer App

Producers will be empowered to Sell to the Distributors / Wholesalers. This will help producers to plan their harvest at the right time for the right place to reduce wastage and get Best Price.

Other Features

All stakeholders will enjoy the benefits of Digitalisation along with multiple features like - Credit Management, Inventory Management, Online Payment, Delivery Tracking, Knowledge about Buyers / Sellers.
