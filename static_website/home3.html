A Digital Enabler for Food Supply Chain”

SarvM.AI is a Digital Enabler connecting consumers, sellers, traders and producers to reduce the wastage of food items across India, by digitizing the food supply chain.

How does SarvM.AI solve real-world challenges?


For Traders

If you are a Wholesaler, Aggregator, Retailer or Hawker, communicate to the market what you are looking for, and get rapid reactions from interested farmers or sellers.

For Consumers

As a consumer, just place your order from your selected vendor and relax. Order shall be delivered in stipulated time. Pay only if you consume. You have an option for self pickup.

For Delivery Partners

A aggregated logistics support for all needs across the food supply chain be it local, intra-city, intercity or inter state.
